Classpublic class HalfEdge
InheritanceHalfEdge com.adobe.csawlib.CSHostObject

Directed side of an edge. An edge has two half edges. You can transverse the contours by walking around these half edges. They are ordered. One half edge represents the one side of the edge, the other half edge represents the other side.
